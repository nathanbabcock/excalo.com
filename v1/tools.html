My Tools:

  • Microsoft Windows XP: Sorry Mac users, but Windows rocks!
  • GIMP: A very powerful and free image editor and creator.
  • Mozilla Firefox: The best browser there is. Long live the fox!
  • Terragen: A terrain generation program. Extremely useful in graphics design.
  • Wink: The ultimate tool for everything from taking screenshots to making video tutorials.
  • The Icon Archive: This website has tons of awesome icons
  • Grappa: This neat little Java applet is the creator of the Excalo interface. Check it out!
  • Wood Workshop : This program can generate thousands of wood textures, tailored specifically to your needs.
  • Nero: Nero is the ultimate tool for all your multimedia needs. Photo, video, audio, Nero has it all.
  • McAfee VirusScan Enterprise: My favorite anti-virus program.
  • Google Earth: A fascinating program. Explore any place on the globe!
  • sTile: A great program for generating tilable backgrounds.
  • The Sage: An excellent dictionary and thesaurus. You can download it or use it online.
  • Install Maker: An excellent free program for creating installations. Also check out their patch maker.
  • Lorem Ipsum Generator: Have you ever seen that "Lorem Ipsum Dolor . . . blah blah blah" placeholding text? Well, this is the place where you get it!
  • Find Sounds.com: Thousands and thousands of free sound effects!
  • Apophysis: An amazing flame fractal generator!
