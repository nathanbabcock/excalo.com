Site redesign and relaunch!

December 20th, 2008

After many, many months of inactivity, Excalo has been relaunched with yet another new look. I'm still not completely satisfied with this one, but it will hold until I feel like making a better one. In the mean time, I intend to be improving my design skills by creating free CSS templates, as well as a steady stream of JavaScript/PHP projects. I have several javascripts on the site already, including simpleAJAX, a dead-simple library providing the basics of AJAX interactions, and Greenlight Validation, a useful javascript validation library. Other new additions to the site include comment features and RSS feeds.

More updates will be coming soon!
