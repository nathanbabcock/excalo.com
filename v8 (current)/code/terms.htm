Terms and Conditions

Overview

What I do for you

  • Code you a design tailored to your specifications for free

What you do for me

  • Provide a link back to excalo.com on every pages that uses the design I coded
  • Allow me to use your project in my portfolio, including screenshots, links, and a description

What I don't do for you

  • Maintain your newly coded website for you

Details

Placing an order

When placing an order, using the order form, make sure that you include all the files needed for your design, packaged in a .zip file. These files should include:

  • A file for each page in the format of .psd, .xcf, or layered .png
  • Any non-standard fonts used in your design
  • (optional) An image to be used as a favicon. If it is not in the correct format it will be resized and convert to .ico format

Make sure you thoroughly fill out all the options on the order form and include all additional instructions in the notes section.

Reasons an order may be rejected

Because this service is offered for free, I am very picky about which orders I will accept. Reasons I may reject an order include, but are not limited to:

  • Aesthetics - This is the primary reason a design will be rejected. I will only code designs that have at least a reasonable degree of polish and style
  • Copyright Infringement - If any part of your design violates copyright laws it will be rejected immediately. This extends to all elements of the design, including images, fonts, and even text content.
  • Offensive Content - If your design incorporates offensive content I may choose to reject it without warning

NOTE: Although I may reject orders if I am aware of copyright infringement, I do not accept any responsibility for said copyright infringements.

Accepted Orders

Within 24 hours of submitting your order, you will recieve an email letting you know whether your order has been accepted or not. It will also list you an estimate of how long the order will take to complete. If it has been accepted I will immediately begin working, unless I need confirmation of your preferences on some issue. Make sure you are explicit in your instructions when ordering so that I can begin working as soon as possible.

Completed Orders

Once an order is completed, I will send you an email with the finished product attached. You may review my work and ask for changes if necessary. After you are satisfied, your project will be added to my portfolio, including screenshots, links, and a description. If/when you use the design I coded for you, you must put a link back to excalo.com. The link needs to be present on every page that uses the design I coded, and it should be easily visible.

Tech Support

If you come back to me later with problems or questions, I will help you provided that your requests are reasonable. Some examples of reasonable requests are:

  • Fixing a mistake that I made
  • Making minor changes to the coded design

Some examples of unreasonable requests are:

  • Asking me to maintain a website for you
  • Asking me to code additional pages - I may or may not do this for you, depending on how busy I am. This request would be the last on my list, though.

Also, if I discover that you have violated the terms and conditions by removing the link back to excalo.com, I will not help you.

If you would like to submit a tech support request, you can either use the contact form or email me directly.
