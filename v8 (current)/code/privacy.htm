Privacy Policy

Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Any artwork provided with your order will only be used during the development of your order. All the copyrights associated with your artwork remain yours. Your artwork will never be resold, given to anyone, or in any way reused without your permission. The only exception to this is that a screenshot, link, and description of your project will be put in the portfolio after your order is completed.

All information you share, including artwork, source files, contact information, and client notes, will always remain confidential.

I will never contact your clients, or the clients of your clients. I respect your business.

What information do you collect?

Throughout the entire excalo.com domain, and also within email communication, we collect the following information:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Order-related information (source files, client notes)
  • Email Transactions
  • IP Address
  • Referrer Information via Cookies

What is my information used for?

Your name, email address, and order-related information will be used during the completion of your order. Email transactions will be saved for reference purposes, but never shared in any way. IP addresses will be collected so that malicious users can be banned from using the site's order or contact form. This is the only time your IP address will be used.

Information about which website referred you here will be collected for the purpose of better understanding how people find this website.

Is my information secure?

Your data security is taken very seriously. The proper physical and electronic barriers have been put in place to protect your information.

How can I change my information?

If you need to correct or change any of your personally identifiable information, you can simply send an email via the contact form.
